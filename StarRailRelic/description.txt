本mod将崩坏星穹铁道中的遗器，角色，武器，Boss等移植进了泰拉瑞亚中。
你可以尽情搭配遗器属性和套装效果来提高自己的实力。
同时可以在泰拉瑞亚中挑战你的老对手们。

注意：作者比较懒，有时候会长时间不更新。

This mod integrates elements from Honkai: Star Rail—including Relics, characters, weapons, and bosses—into Terraria. 
Freely mix and match Relic stats and set bonuses to power up your build.
And challenge your old foes in the world of Terraria.

Note: The developer may take extended breaks between updates.
